TAIWAN SEMICONDUCTOR MANUFACTURING CO.>

NASDAQ: TSM


Key Facts:

  1. About 60% of all microchips – and more than 90% of the more advanced types – are made in Taiwan, largely by the Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company.

Key takeaways:

  1. Taiwan is a key part of the global supply chain for tech.
  2. Conflict between China and Taiwan could disrupt global economic stability:

    Mark Williams, chief Asia economist at consultancy Capital Economics, says: “War between China and Taiwan would deliver a shock to the global economy that would be bigger than anything we’ve seen in modern times.”
    Research by Nikkei Asia finds $2.6 trillion dollars woulf evaporate from the world economy if the U.S., Europe and Japan imposed sanctions on China to contain its economy, as they did to Russia over its invasion of Ukraine.
