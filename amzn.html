Amazon.com Inc.

NASDAQ: AMZN

E-Commerce • Cloud Computing • Digital Services

Price Chart

Key Facts

Retail & Cloud Leader

Amazon is the world’s largest online retailer and a dominant force in cloud computing through AWS.

Prime Ecosystem

Amazon Prime membership drives customer loyalty and recurring revenue across retail, streaming, and more.

Key Takeaways

Margin Pressure

Amazon faces margin pressure from logistics costs and heavy investment in new businesses.

Cloud Growth

AWS continues to be a major profit driver, with strong growth in enterprise cloud adoption.

Investment Summary

Amazon’s scale, innovation, and cloud leadership make it a key player in global commerce and technology. Investors should watch for margin trends and regulatory developments.
